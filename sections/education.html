Education

I was born on February 20, 2000, in San Benedetto del Tronto, a coastal city in Italy, where I grew up and lived until I was 18.

I started elementary school at just five years old, a year earlier than usual, at my parents' wish so that I could attend the same school as my sister.
After five years, I moved on to middle school and then to the science high school in my hometown, where my passion for physics and mathematics blossomed.

At 18, I left home to move to Milan and study Computer Engineering at the Politecnico. During the pandemic, I went through challenging times when loneliness and the lack of social interaction began to weigh on me. During those periods, I felt unmotivated and realized that I needed to focus on getting to know myself as well as on my studies.

In 2021, I graduated and decided to continue for another year at the Politecnico, concentrating on Artificial Intelligence. However, I was unsure whether to stay in Milan or try an experience abroad. So, as I worked to clarify my goals, I applied to EPFL.
