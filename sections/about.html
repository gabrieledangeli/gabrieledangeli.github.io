About

I have always been fascinated by understanding how things work: once I understood the importance of my sensitivity, I dedicated myself to analyzing my feelings, trying to understand their causes, from the most superficial to the deepest ones. I wanted to live in harmony, and even today, this quest represents my greatest inspiration.

During those same years, while studying, I was learning how things work. What I loved about science was its elegance, its rigorous method, and the illusion it offered of being able to control nature. I wanted to combine what I was learning to weave a unique tale of the world.

In art, I see a perfect synthesis between the search for answers and the celebration of questions: to my eyes, it seems to unite the spiritual and the scientific.
